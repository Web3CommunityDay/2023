Ask Question?

Web 3 Community Day

How do I register for the event?
You can register for the event online at the event website under pricing section.
Is the ticket transferrable?
We understand that sometimes things come up and you may not be able to attend the event. However, we have a strict no-transfer policy in place to ensure that all tickets are sold to attendees who are genuinely interested in attending the event.
Can I register and pay at the event?
It is recommended that you register online in advance to save time and ensure your spot.
Where is the event being held?
The event venue is Eshan College Of Engineering, Farah, Mathura that listed on the event website. You can also use Google Maps to get directions to the venue..
What should I do if I have a problem during the event?
If you have a technical problem during the event, please contact the event staff. They will be able to help you troubleshoot the problem and get you back to the event.
Can I get a refund if I cannot attend the event?
We understand that sometimes things come up and you may not be able to attend the event. However, we have a strict no-refund policy in place to ensure that we can cover the costs of the event, such as speaker fees, venue rental, and catering.
What are the different types of tickets available?
There are a variety of ticketing options available, depending on your needs. We offer early bird Ticket, general Ticket, and late bird Ticket.
Is there a networking event?
Yes, there is a networking event on the last day of the event. This is a great opportunity to meet other attendees and learn more about their work.
